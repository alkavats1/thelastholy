      • What are the things which one should keep in mind while performing Asthi Visarjan at Haridwar Ghat?

  • Asthi of the deceased body should be collected in an urn or a cloth after the cremation and those should be preserved for 10 days. On the 11th day, the close family members should perform this ritual.
  • Before the asthi visarjan, family members should perform pind daan and shraadh. After that, they should perform asthi prwaah or asthi visarjan.
  • Asthi visarjan ritual should be started with the holy Vedic mantras chanting in a loud and clear voice.
  • It is believed that if the asthi visarjan ritual is not performed in a suitable manner or at a suitable place then the departed soul never becomes happy.
