OUR ASTHI VISARJAN SERVICES

Asthi Visarjan is a Hindu rite in which you immerse yourself in your loved one’s ashes and bones. According to Hindu texts, sprinkling a loved one’s ashes in holy waters can free their spirit from enslavement and assist them in achieving emancipation.


The Last Holy Journey staff will assist you in executing the Asthi Visarjan services at the Holy Water by assisting you in selecting the appropriate Pandit, Samagri, and Puja, as well as ensuring your comfort.
